### 1.1 Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
EduSync uses strict permissions to protect data:
- **Owners**: Manage multiple schools and global users.
- **Admins**: Manage school configuration, students, staff, and finances.
- **Accountants**: Focus on fee generation, collection, and financial reports.
- **Teachers**: Manage attendance, results, and student behavior.
- **Parents/Students**: View profiles, academic progress, and fee status.

## 4. üí∞ Financial Management (Fees)
### 4.1 The Ledger Logic
EduSync uses a "Balance-Forward" system. 
- **Arrears**: If a student owes money from previous months, it automatically carries forward to the next month's challan.

### 4.2 Recording Payments
Use **Fee Management > Fee Collection** or the **Scan & Pay** camera tool.
- **Partial Payments**: You can record multiple small payments against one challan.

## 5. ‚òÅÔ∏è Data Backup & Safety
### 5.1 Google Drive Cloud Backup (NEW)
You can now save your school's data to your personal Google Drive account.
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Find the **Cloud Backup** section.
3. Click **Backup to Drive**. A Google login popup will appear.
4. Authorize EduSync to save files. A JSON backup will be created in your Drive.
5. To restore, click **Restore from Drive**, and select one of your previous backups from the list.

### 5.2 Offline vs Online Sync
- **Offline-First**: Recommended. All changes are saved locally instantly and synced to Neon in the background. Works even if your internet drops for a few minutes.
- **Online-Only**: Recommended for offices with multiple staff members working simultaneously on different computers to avoid conflicts.
